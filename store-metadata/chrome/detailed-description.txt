Lost in a sea of browser tabs? Organize them like you think.

Tab Canopy brings tree-based organization to your browser tabs. Unlike traditional tab managers that use flat lists or simple groups, Tab Canopy lets you nest tabs hierarchically - perfect for research, development workflows, and complex projects.
